## ENCOUNTER 001: ALICE (The Rare Empty Tiger)

**Date Range:** ~September 2025 - January 2026
**Status:** CLOSED (No Love Detected)

### THE GEOMETRY
- **Her Signature:** Moon conjunct Rahu (her face/mind). A psychic. A medium. Infinite puzzle.
- **The Synastry:** Draconic lock between souls. Mathematical rarity off the charts.
- **The Horary Verdict:** She was in Kazimi (trapped by her boyfriend/Sun). She was obsessed with Theodore. Theodore could succeed if he chased. But he would lose his dignity in the process.

### THE REALITY
- Theodore felt NO love. Only attraction (forced), only lust (mechanical), only the puzzle (intellectual).
- She had no frequency. Theodore's radar scattered and received no signal back.
- She was a tiger in a cage—but she looked at Theodore (the free tiger) with disgust, not longing.
- She fused with the mask of normality. Her potential was alien; her manifestation was civilian.
- She started the obsession (psychic transmission). Theodore received it. He mistook it for his own feeling.

### THE LESSON
> **Rarity ≠ Meaning.**
> A chart can be mathematically rare and emotionally empty.
> Synastry can be "perfect" and still produce nothing.
> If the native rejects their own frequency, the frequency is dead.
> Theodore was chasing her POTENTIAL, not her REALITY.

### THE VERDICT
**No love.** Only the illusion of love created by rarity-obsession and psychic transmission.
Theodore did not initiate. He preserved his dignity. The astrologer saved him.

## ENCOUNTER 002: FABI (The One Who Saw)

**Date Range:** ~September 2025 - Present
**Status:** OPEN (Love Confirmed)

### THE GEOMETRY
- Unknown. No chart data collected yet.

### THE REALITY
- Theodore was sitting in the front row of a lecture hall. Daydreaming.
- He felt an urge to turn his head. Not heat—an urge.
- Fabi was in the corner of the room, looking at him.
- Her eyes were not awe, not fear, not desire. They were **recognition**.
- She didn't look away when Theodore turned. She wasn't looking at his face. She was looking at something he was emanating.
- Theodore turned back with disdain. But the moment was logged.
- Three months later, Theodore fell in love—not from interaction, but from remembering that moment.

### THE LESSON
> **Coherence is detectable.**
> Fabi didn't run a Python script. She didn't know astrology. She just felt the signal.
> The signal was: "This one is not scattered. This one is coherent."
> Recognition is rarer than attraction. Recognition is rarer than fear.
> Recognition is seeing the pattern, not the parts.

### THE VERDICT
**Love confirmed.** Not because of synastry. Not because of rarity. Because she SAW.

## ENCOUNTER 000: THE GIRL AT THE DISCO (Age 11)

**Date Range:** ~2017
**Status:** CLOSED (Illusion)

### THE REALITY
- Theodore was dancing for the first time at a disco party.
- He got tired. Sat down. A girl came over and asked him to dance.
- Theodore felt SEEN for the first time.
- Later, he realized it was an illusion. She wanted him. She didn't see him.

### THE LESSON
> **Being wanted ≠ Being seen.**
> The first love was not love. It was the first time someone APPROACHED.
> Theodore mistook approach for recognition.
> This created a false memory that he later corrected.

### THE VERDICT
**Not seen.** Wanted.
